"Don't Throw Out My Lego"

  • Post by Kimberley Masuku
  • Aug 23, 2020

"Peterpan Syndrome" is a term used to describe a psychological condition in which the "sufferer" does not want to grow up and would rather remain a child, much like Peter Pan who wished to remain young forever.

It only makes sense to want to revert to a simpler time, when we didn't have anxieties about money, responsibilties, finding love, and gaining acceptance in society. When our only responsibility was to simply just BE.

In a way, this blog and my art is giant middle finger to the expectations of adulthood. It's an attempt to hold on to the best parts of childhood, and to relive and revive the feelings of HOPE and BOLDNESS they inspire.

Towards the end of last year, a friend of mine got me to listen to a song called "Dont Throw Out My Legos" by the band "AJR" . It's about a young man who's off to live on his own for the first time. He's experiencing mixed feelings because although he's craved independence and exploration, he knows the world isn't always kind and so he wants to hold on to his childhood. Which is represented by Lego (or legos if you're American)

The song immediately resonated with me and it became my anthem. As playing with Lego was a huuge part of my childhood and it always made me feel really creative and like I could do anything. I played the song when I went home for the December holidays, and it surprisingly also struck a chord with my siblings despite the fact they are well-adjusted individuals who don't have a preoccupation with childhood and eternal youth.

Anyway, my birthday is coming up soon and if you love me, you'll get me this novelty lego mug.

That's what this is entire post was about... A mug... Get me the Mug.
