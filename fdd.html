The Waters of Madness

  • Post by Kimberley Masuku
  • Aug 23, 2020

A story is told about a kingdom where the royal court uncovered a plot to poison the king. The conspirators had tainted the water in the king's well with poison they obtained from the most feared witch in all the land in a bid to drive him completely mad.

Now, although the king's sanity was preserved because the plot was uncovered in time, his advisors forgot an important detail. At the start of the drought months before, the king in all his benevolence had given the towns people permission to drink from his own well.

This meant that before the day was up, all the townspeople had drunk from the contaminated well and become crazy. The king tried to speak sense to them but they accused him of being crazy and wanted to kill him for it. The king then decided to dribk from the well of the waters of madness so that he coukd engage the people from their level. Which he did nd retained control of his kingdom.

THe point of this story is this: There are no perfect people, only people who have mastered/ are suited to certain environments. For the longest time, I was incredibly hard on myself for failing to fit in to certain environments, I internalized that failure and saw it as evidence that there must be something wrong with me. But the liberating truth is; different people flourish in different conditions. And

as far as fitting in and finding acceptance/ love and real frinds goes, this will only be possible if you're around people who drink from the same waters (take in the same content/ subscribe to the same philosophies) as you.

And if you haven't found your tribe yet, don't worry, it will happen naturally and in good time
