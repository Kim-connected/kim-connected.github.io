What Inspired The Name?

It became apparent to me that I predominantly operate from a place of unstructuredness. A trait that some might call "chaotic" or "messy". I don't have things all figured out and I'm done pretending to and being hard on myself because I finally understand that our biggest weaknesses are also our greatest strengths when put at the service of our goals and purposes. So, i decided to embrace the messiness instead of swimming against the current, and this blog is the result.

What Spectacular Mess is:

* A Collection of Letters to My Sister and Myself - because this is cheaper than therapy

* My creative outlet

* A form of rebellion against conventionl adulthood

* An endeavor to capture the essence of who i am... -in case I ever forget

* My way of leaving my mark in a world that could otherwise forget me and treat my life with oblivion

* A passion Project

* A Labour of Love

* My expressive fingerprint/ DNA
