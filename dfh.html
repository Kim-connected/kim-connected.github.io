Dear Future Husband

  • Post by Kimberley Masuku
  • Aug 23, 2020

Dear Future husband, lately I find myself looking at objects and cosndiering how they make me feel and whether or not they resonate with my spirit. Some days when I sit in the work shuttle, I look out the window and just allow myself to take eveyrthing in. and if they make I've looking at houses,. I like old houses, not VIctorian old, but old as in: "lived-in", I like the type of house that speak of/ look like you could raise a happy family in them. Big enough for eveyrone, kids, husband and myself to have space to do what we need to do. bIg enough yard and garden for the kids to play in and ride their bicycles around. Enough space inside not to feel claustrophobic. but also not so big that it feels alienating or becomes a chore to manage. I"m thinking that our furniture choices, while obviously aestheic, should also be minimalistic and practical. I want absolute freedom in our home. I once visted a family friends house where the sofas were still in plastic packaging even after 20+ years and where the kids hadn't been allowed to sit on the soas up until they were well into their teens. While I am no one's judge and jury, I couldn't help but think it oppressive.

Mum : ok...
Also Mum: *Gets me a doll ( without hair to make matters worse!!!)

Me at age 14: Please get me school shoes like Vusi's, I'm definitely not a fan o fthose chunky ones, especially since I'm short and hav the longest uniform in the school.
Mum: okay...
ALso mum: * gets me Wayne brenner School shoes

We learn about people's contributions in history, we learn about army generals and great svientists whose contributions have adnaced the cause of the human race. We learn about a few other people too who have acheived fame and status

I understand that it makes more sense to allow you get to know me naturally , but I also understand that I'm incredibly dynamic and I don't want this version of myself to be lost without being ackonwledged and celevrated. I feel beautiful and I love this version myself. I will probably love the versions to come, but this one is paving hte way for all furture versions. She's a gem. Her favourite things in the world to do are PLAY and CREATE. I write this down so that it will remind me who I am if /i ever forget.

To get to that I ill use the process of elimination, to see what doesn't move me at all and rule that out. "Dont Throw Out My Legos" by the band "AJR" [ check it out ]. It's about a young man who's off to live on his own for the first time. He's experiencing mixed feelings because although he's craved independence and exploration, he knows the world isn't always kind and so he wants to hold on to his childhood. Which is represented by Lego (or legos if you're American)

My desire is that we would alwyas operate from a place of self-knowledge and understanding and kindness. To do only that which is in alignment with what we believe and aspire to.

Anyway, my birthday is coming up soon and if you love me, you'll get me this novelty lego mug.

That's what this is entire post was about... A mug... Get me the Mug.
